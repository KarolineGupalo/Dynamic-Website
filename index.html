About me

Hi! I'm Ciri the brown labrador. I am almost 3 years old but still called a 'baby' by my pawrents. Basically I live for snacks and treats and I try to get a taste of everything my hoomans eat. To this point you'd probably expect me to talk about my job, but the fact is, that I am stay-at-home dog, who doesn't even protect the house too much, since I just love everyone. Sometimes I bark at things that are new to me, like snow men or so.

Follow me on instagram for even more adventures with me and my hoomans!
